About

Tony Coop

A web industry professional since 2005, beginning with 3 years in research and development, followed by a progression into front-end development in 2008. I take great pride in what I do.

Advanced HTML5 and CSS3 (including LESS and SASS)
Intermediate/advanced vanilla JavaScript as well as frameworks like jQuery & Underscore.
Experienced in producing responsive layouts.
Advanced templating with PHP, Moustache and Blade.
Advanced theming using CMSs including Drupal (v6) and Modx.
Advanced design, coding and best practice of HTML for email.
Good knowledge of Photoshop, Illustrator and Fireworks.
Excellent understanding of mobile web development, web standards, accessibility, usability and SEO.
